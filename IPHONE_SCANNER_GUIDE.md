## Troubleshooting

**"Camera not opening?"**
- Refresh the page once
- Make sure Safari has camera permission
- Try tapping the blue button again

**"QR not detected?"**
- Make sure QR code fills most of the frame
- Ensure guest's screen brightness is high
- Hold steady for 1 second
